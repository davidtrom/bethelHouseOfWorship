Devoted to Prayer

Life is full of challenges that can feel overwhelming. However, you don't have to face them alone. God is always by your side to give you strength in your darkest moments.

Bethel House of Worship Prayer Line Altar in Dover, Delaware is here for those in need so that they may feel the divine presence of the Lord. We help all of our brothers and sisters, no matter who they are or where they come from.

What We Do

Bethel House of Worship Prayer Line Altar, prays with groups of people who come for prayer every Friday, Sunday, Wednesday 10:00 PM to 11:15 PM.

Sunday: Prayer of Thanksgiving and Blessings
Wednesday: Prayer Over the Kingdom of Darkness
Friday: Holy Ghost Prayer and Speaking in Tongues
